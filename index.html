Longfei Shangguan

Assistant Professor

University of Pittsburgh

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science, University of Pittsburgh. My lab builds mobile, embedded, and networking systems for fun.

Current projects

  • Earable computing: hardware platform, software stack, and mobile applications.
  • No power to transmit: backscatter communication and sensing.
  • Small is the next big: machine learning on resource constrained devices.
  • Taming bio-inspired sensors with bio-inspired system designs: hardware and software solutions for event cameras.
