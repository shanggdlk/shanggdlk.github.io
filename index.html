Longfei Shangguan

Assistant Professor

University of Pittsburgh

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Computer Science, University of Pittsburgh. My research interests lie in IoT, mobile, and wireless systems, from applications, security, networking, all the way down to the PHY-layer&Hardware architecture.

Current projects

  • Earable computing: hardware platform, software stack, and mobile applications.
  • No power to transmit: backscatter communication and sensing.
  • Small is next big: machine learning on resource constrained devices.

Research Interests

  • Ultra-low power communication
  • Mobile and (w)earable systems
  • IoT and edge computing
  • Machine learning in the wild
